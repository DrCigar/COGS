CALCULATIONS
Avg YoY Change
Mean year-over-year percentage change across all materials in your catalog.
Sourced from published pricing reports comparing current vs. 12-month-prior values.

YoY Breadth
Count of materials rising vs. falling year-over-year. Helps gauge broad cost pressure.
Format: Rising / Falling. "Majority rising" if more than half are up YoY.

Risk Items
C = Critical: materials with YoY > 50% or flagged critical impact.
H = High: materials flagged as high supply chain impact.

Supply Chain Risk Level
Based on average YoY across your catalog:
  > 30% = CRITICAL  |  > 15% = ELEVATED  |  > 5% = MODERATE  |  ≤ 5% = NORMAL

Optimism Meters
Forward-looking sentiment score (0-100) per category based on analyst intel,
supply forecasts, and your own notes. Editable per category.
0 = Very Bearish (costs staying high) → 100 = Very Bullish (relief expected).

Pricing Data
All baseline prices and YoY% are from real-world sources (April 2026).
Sources include TrendForce, Trading Economics, Metalary, ChemOrbis, and J2 Sourcing.
Prices are static snapshots — updated manually on request.
Sparkline charts show the 12-month trend from prior-year price to current baseline.

Alert Thresholds
Critical: fires when any material's YoY% exceeds the critical threshold.
Warning: fires when YoY% exceeds warning but below critical.
Each alert fires once per material to avoid spam.
